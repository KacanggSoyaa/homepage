    Semester 2

    On my Second semester , I was still active in the Kelab Teknologi Digital and I also still the AJK Multimedia on the Multimedia Biro. I was assigned to be the AJK Multimedia for the second time. Not gonna lie I was so happy to be the AJK Multimedia again because I can learn more about multimedia and also can improve my skills in multimedia. Not just that I've improved my communication skills after joining the club, Before this I was a shy person and not really good at communicating with people. But after joining the club, I can talk to people more confidently and fluently. Second semester was the big changes in my diploma life because I was given a lot of task and responsibilities. I also got a chance to joined one of the outside program that have been organized by Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC). 2 representative of Kelab Teknologi Digital was chosen to joined the program and I was one of them. A lot of representative of other clubs from the different polytechnic and university was there and I was so excited to meet them. The program was held for 1 days at Cyberjaya. I learned a lot of things from the program and also met with a lot of new friends. I was so grateful to be one of the representative of Kelab Teknologi Digital to joined the program.

    On my Third semester , I was appointed to be the Vice President of Kelab Teknologi Digital. I was so happy to be the Vice President because I can learn more about leadership and also can improve my skills in leadership. This semester is quite challenging because I need to handle a lot of programs and also need to manage my time well for focusing on studying. I also need to make sure that all the programs that have been planned can be executed successfully. I will make sure that I can balance my time between club activities and studies. And guess what, I did it! I managed to get a good result for my semester 3 final exam. I was so happy and grateful for that. This is the proof that if you work hard and never give up, you can achieve your goals.
